OVERVIEW
doom released in 1993 is a fps from ID software who practically gave birth to the pc gaming industry after successfully implementing side scrolling in their game Commader keen during a time when pc graphic cards did not have to the power to have moving backgrounds after commander keen id released the grand father of the fps genre wolfenstein 3d witch successfully had 3d graphics on a pc where 2 years prior side scrolling wasn't possible wolfenstien used a method called ray casting witch is the act of casting rays from the players position and direction and recording what wall the ray hits and drawing its texture and distance on screen making it look 3d paired with keens side scrolling the game gained immense popularity a year later id release doom witch became possibly one of the most influential pc games of all time doom featured height and allowed the player to move up and down in the level doom also had different rooms having windows into each other witch wasn't possible in wolfenstein the new ground breaking innovations combined with dooms addicting game play loop made the game developers millionaires in their 20s by 1998 doom sold 1.36 million copies and earned $8.74million

REVIEW
in doom you play as a super soldier space marine on a martian base that has been invaded by hell doom doesn't really have any story except for a text paragraph at the end of episodes doom gained popularity due to its game play loop where you run at break neck speeds around in a hell invaded facility shooting demons when you run out of bullets use a chain saw or punch them in the face doom also has a power up the makes your bunches kill and obliterate demons in a single punch when you kill demons the bodies get left on the floor showing you where you've already been by the blood trail if you kill a demon hard enough the disintegrate into blood the entire game is very gory witch also helped to its popularity due to the alternatives being child and family friendly games from Nintendo and Sega witch was very appealing to adults and teens

INFLUENCE
doom practically gave birth to the fps genre and made major technological strides in the pc world after doom id made quake witch was the first game to feature full 3d with actual 3d models and the ability to look up and down as traverse a 3d space with rooms on top of one another quake does not have the same legacy as doom due to it being more Erie and less rocking out to ac-dc style music while punching demons however quake did eventually lead to valves half life witch made the pc industry how it is today in conclusion doom has a big legacy and player base to this day and gave birth to the fps genre witch is what founding the pc gaming industry
