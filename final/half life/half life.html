OVERVIEW half life released in 1998 is a fps built on a heavily modified version of the id software's QUAKE engine witch the studio named the gold source engine half life was the first fps game to feature a story the game was named Novembers 6th best selling pc game by 1999 half life sold 212,173 copies and earned $8.6mil

half life ended up being a very influential game for the fps genre and pc game industry. half life flipped the fps genre from a shoot em up and made puzzles tactical decisions story skeletal animation and responsive world a normal occurence in the fps genre. and the developer valve went on to be possible the biggest and most customer friendly company in the pc gaming industry after half life valve made many more ground breaking games valve also made steam witch turned in to the amazon for digital pc game distribution valve also made strides in the vr world featuring their own headsets and games as well as the console world by providing the steam machine when Microsoft tried to take over the industry useing windows and more recently the steam deck for a portable experience without resorting to a lap top
